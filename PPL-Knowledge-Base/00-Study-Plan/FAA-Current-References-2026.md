## How To Keep This Current

Run this quick check monthly (or before your knowledge test/checkride):
1. Confirm ACS version and publication date.
2. Confirm AIM current cycle effective date.
3. Confirm chart cycle date.
4. Confirm handbook addenda dates.
5. Update note `updated:` field and any affected concept notes.

## High-Risk Topics to Re-Verify Every Time

- Medical duration and BasicMed eligibility.
- Passenger currency and night currency windows.
- VFR weather minimums by airspace and altitude.
- Equipment/INOP dispatch logic under Part 91.
- Fuel reserve requirements and practical planning margins.
